To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page. Fill in your basic information, create a password, and submit. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Once signed in, go to your dashboard and select the “List a Property” option. Follow the prompts to add details, upload photos, and set your price or rent terms. After submitting, your listing will appear on our site.

There are no fees for browsing or signing up. However, a small service fee may apply when listing a property for sale or rent. The exact fee will be shown during the listing process.
